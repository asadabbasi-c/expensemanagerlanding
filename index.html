✦ Features

Everything you need to manage money

Powerful tools built for everyday use — no account, no cloud, no compromise on privacy.

📱

Smart Expense Tracking

Add expenses in seconds. Organize by category, merchant, and date with full search, filters, and month-by-month history.

📷

Receipt Scanner (OCR)

Point your camera at any receipt. On-device ML Kit OCR extracts the amount, merchant, and date automatically — no internet needed.

🎙️

Voice Expense Entry

Just say "500 food at KFC" and SmartSpend logs it instantly. Hands-free expense tracking while driving, cooking, or shopping.

📩

SMS Auto-Import

Automatically reads bank debit SMS messages and imports them as expenses — zero manual entry for every card transaction.

⭐ Pro Feature
📊

Analytics & Charts

Pie charts and bar graphs reveal exactly where your money goes. Tap any category to drill down into individual transactions.

🎯

Budget Goals & Tracking

Set monthly income and savings targets. SmartSpend tracks your progress and warns you before you go over budget.

🔄

Recurring Expenses

Set Netflix, rent, or any subscription as recurring. SmartSpend tracks due dates and shows your total monthly commitments.

📤

Export & Import Data

Export all expenses to CSV or JSON for spreadsheet analysis. Import back anytime — your data is always yours.

🔒

100% Offline & Private

All data lives on your device. No account, no server, no cloud. SmartSpend works with zero internet connection.
