Who we are

Walletly is a personal finance and expense tracking application developed as an independent Android app. References to "we", "us", or "our" in this policy refer to the Walletly developer.

This Privacy Policy explains how Walletly handles information when you use our Android application. By using Walletly, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information we collect

Walletly is designed with a local-first, privacy-first architecture. The app does not require any account, sign-in, or personal identification.

📱 Data stored on your device only

All expense records, categories, budget goals, settings, and preferences are stored in a local database on your device. This data never leaves your phone unless you explicitly export it.

Data you create within the app:

  • Expense entries (amounts, dates, categories, merchants, descriptions)
  • Category names, icons, and colors you create
  • Budget goals and monthly income figures
  • Currency preference and app settings
  • Recurring expense schedules
  • Exported data files (CSV/JSON) that you save to your device

Data we do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Account credentials (no account exists)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers beyond what AdMob requires (see Section 5)
  • Contact information
  • Photos or receipt images (these are processed on-device and discarded)
  • Voice recordings (speech-to-text is processed on-device via Android's built-in API)

App permissions explained

Walletly requests only the permissions necessary for its features. Here is what each permission is used for:

Permission Why it's needed Status
📷 Camera To capture photos of receipts for OCR scanning. Images are processed on-device using ML Kit and are never uploaded. Optional
📩 Read SMS To scan incoming bank debit messages and suggest automatic expense entries (Pro feature only). Messages are read locally and never transmitted. Optional · Pro
🎙️ Microphone To enable voice-based expense entry ("500 food at KFC"). Speech is transcribed on-device via Android's RecognizerIntent API. Optional
🌐 Internet Required only to load banner advertisements (AdMob) for free-tier users, and to process the Pro upgrade purchase via Google Play Billing. Required
⚠️ SMS Permission (Pro only)

If you grant SMS permission, Walletly reads messages only from your local inbox to identify bank transaction patterns. No message content is ever sent to any server. You can revoke this permission at any time in your Android Settings → Apps → Walletly → Permissions.

How we use information

All information processing in Walletly happens entirely on your device. Specifically:

  • Expense data is stored in a local Room database to power your expense history, charts, and reports
  • Receipt photos are passed to the on-device ML Kit OCR engine to extract text. The image is not saved after processing unless you confirm the expense
  • Voice recordings are passed to Android's built-in speech recognition. Walletly only receives the transcribed text result, not the audio
  • SMS messages (Pro only) are scanned locally using pattern matching to detect bank debit transactions. Raw message content is never stored or transmitted
  • Currency preference is stored in SharedPreferences on your device

We do not use your information for advertising profiling, analytics reporting, machine learning training, or any purpose beyond powering the app features you use.

Third-party services

Walletly integrates with the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy that governs how they handle data.

📢 Google AdMob (Free users only)

Free-tier users see banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (advertising ID), IP address, and ad interaction data to serve relevant ads. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Pro users see no ads and AdMob is not loaded for them.

💳 Google Play Billing

The Pro upgrade is processed entirely by Google Play. Walletly only receives a purchase confirmation token — we never handle your payment information directly. This is governed by Google Payments Privacy Notice.

🔤 Google ML Kit (On-device)

Receipt scanning uses ML Kit Text Recognition, which runs entirely on-device. No images or text are sent to Google's servers. This is governed by ML Kit Terms of Service.

Data storage and security

Your expense data is stored in a SQLite database on your device, managed by Android's Room persistence library. The database is stored in your app's private data directory, which is only accessible to Walletly (and to you via Android's backup features or manual export).

  • Data is stored in your app's private storage, inaccessible to other apps
  • No data is backed up to our servers — there are no servers
  • Android's built-in backup (Google Drive) may back up app data depending on your device settings — this is controlled by you in Android Settings
  • You can export your data as CSV or JSON at any time from Settings → Export Data
  • Uninstalling Walletly permanently deletes all locally stored data
⚠️ No cloud recovery

Because we store no data on servers, we cannot recover your data if your device is lost or damaged without a prior export or Android backup. We recommend using the Export feature regularly.

Your rights and choices

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access your data — All your expense data is visible within the app at all times
  • Export your data — Settings → Export Data to save as CSV or JSON
  • Delete your data — Uninstall the app to permanently delete all data. You can also clear app data via Android Settings → Apps → Walletly → Clear Data
  • Revoke permissions — You can revoke Camera, SMS, or Microphone permissions at any time in Android Settings without affecting core expense tracking functionality
  • Opt out of personalized ads — You can reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads in Android Settings → Google → Ads
  • Remove ads entirely — Upgrading to Pro removes all advertisements

Children's privacy

Walletly is a personal finance application intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from or direct services to children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction).

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used Walletly, please contact us and we will assist with deletion of any locally stored data. Since the app requires no account, uninstalling the app removes all data entirely.
